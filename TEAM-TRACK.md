# Team track (optional)

If 1–2 other people are doing this program at the same time:

- **Review each other's PRs** — at least 3 comments each, focused on *why*, not *what*.
- Do not share fixes before the other person has formed their own hypothesis.
- For the discovery bug (Bug 3), do **not** tell each other where it is. Compare *how* you each
  found it afterwards — the investigation path is the lesson, not the answer.
- After the module, write two sentences: where did you disagree on an approach, and how did you
  resolve it?

This is optional, but reviewing someone else's reasoning is one of the fastest ways to sharpen
your own. It is not required to advance.
